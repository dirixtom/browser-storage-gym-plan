Months 4–6
Building
The split widens to three workouts (legs+biceps / push / pull) and weights step up meaningfully. Bulgarian split squats enter, plus rear delt fly and face pulls to balance shoulder development. Volume drops slightly per exercise; intensity rises.

Months 7–9
Intensity
Heavier straight sets with full 2-minute rests. Same 3-workout split, fewer working sets per exercise but considerably more load. This is where strength gains start showing visibly. Sessions are longer (60–75 min) — that's the trade-off for the heavier work.

Months 10–12
Mastery
Strict tempo on every rep — 3 seconds lowering, 1-second pause, controlled lift. Same exercises as Phase 3 but the slower lowering phase makes lighter weights feel heavy and dramatically increases time under tension. Sessions run 70–85 min. Take a deload week (50% weight, 2 sets) every 6 weeks.
